## How to Check if a Video Has Captions

Before testing, you can manually verify on YouTube:

1. Open the video on YouTube
2. Click the "CC" (Closed Captions) button in the video player
3. If captions appear, the video can be processed
4. Look for "English (auto-generated)" or manual subtitles

## Videos to AVOID

⚠️ **These types of videos often DON'T have captions:**

- Short user-generated content (< 1 minute)
- Very old videos (pre-2015)
- Small channels that disabled auto-captions
- Some TEDx talks from smaller events
- Private or unlisted videos
- Videos with copyright restrictions
